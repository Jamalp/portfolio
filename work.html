Places

Places is my first project. You can search any venue in the world with its corresponding location and Places will display the most recent photos or videos taken there. The photos all come from instagram. You can check out and fork the code on Github. Feel free to email me with any suggestions, ideas or questions.

Explorer

Explorer is a map to help you find the best hotels to stay while on vacation. Use Explorer to explore your surroundings based on the things that interest you and find hotels located in those areas. Explorer was created, built and cared for by Tim Blonski & Jamal Powell. Email us with suggestions, advice and find us on Github!
Happy Exploring.
